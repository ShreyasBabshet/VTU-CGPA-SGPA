How to Calculate SGPA???

Here are few steps to Calculate SGPA

Calculating SGPA is a fairly easy process. Here is how to calculate SGPA:

  • Write down all your credit points for each subject
  • Next, multiply the credit point of each subject with the grade that you achieved in that subject. Each grade covers a range, so you can take the median.
  • Add all the scores that come
  • Divide the total score that you got with the sum of all the credit points
  • Number that you get is the SGPA.
